Clan Directives

1. Activity Requirements

All members must log in at least once every 3 days. If you are going on vacation, post in the #afk-notices Discord channel. Inactivity longer than 5 days without notice results in an auto-kick to make room for active grinders.

2. Clan War Attendance

Level 40+ members are expected to attend Server Wars every Saturday at 8 PM EST. Voice comms are mandatory; you don't have to speak, but you must listen to Raid Leaders.

3. PVP & Rivalry Rules

Do not attack allied clans. However, members of our active rival are Kill On Sight (KOS). Looting rivals is highly encouraged. Toxic behavior in global chat is frowned upon—let your stats do the talking.

4. Vault Loot Distribution

High-tier boss drops belong to the Clan Vault first. They are distributed based on merit, attendance, and current class needs by the Officers. Do not beg for gear.
